We are the Federal Bureau of Control

We protect the nation from altered items and objects of power. Led by Director Jesse Faden, our mission is to collect, understand, and control altered items and objects of power.

About the Bureau

The Federal Bureau of Control is headquarted in New York City, occupying a building known internally as The Oldest House. The Bureu consists of six divisions, each fulfilling necessary tasks to the everyday running of the Bureau. For example, the containment sector houses dangerous objects known as altered items and objects of power. The employees situated in this sector perform very important tasks such as categorizing the altered items and objects of power, performing rituals to calm the altered items and objects of power, and much more.
